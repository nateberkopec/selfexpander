"Some of the greatest joys in my life are the things I used to hate, or know nothing about, and now have grown to love.

So I thought: Instead of letting it happen accidently or randomly, why not be deliberate about it?"

-Derek Sivers, "Monthly Self-Expansion Project"

What is the Self-Expander?

The Self-Expander suggests a random Very Short Introduction or a Great Course.

It was inspired by Derek Sivers' post, linked above, about expanding one's horizons by learning about things you may not have any interest in. Thus, you may only use the Self-Expander once per week, to encourage you to actually experience the randomness and not simply use the Self-Expander until you find a topic you are already interested in.
